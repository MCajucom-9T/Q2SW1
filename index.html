Flora
Flora is the most soft-spoken out of all the members. She is portrayed as a very calm and caring person. Most see her as the most mature person in the group; her only downside is the fact that she tends to hide her feelings. Despite this, her personality is sensitive in nature. This is seen by how much she cares about her plants and nature- often talking to them as well. In line with this, she is the fairy of nature.

Tecna
Tecna, as the name suggests, is the fairy of technology. She was described at the start of the show as someone who seemed like a robot, not conveying much feelings. This is something she learns to change as she continues living her best life with her friends at the winx club. Soon, she becomes one of the most emotionally inclined members of the group. Aside from this, she is extremely smart and logical; however, this- at times- can prove to be a bit irritating.
